My Hobbies

Coding

One of my most enjoyable pastimes is writing code for computer programs. Whenever I am feeling unmotivated, I can currently fall back on this safe space. Not only does it improve my condition, but it has also enabled me to experiment with a wide range of techniques.

Sleeping

After I've finished my other hobbies, I usually go to sleep. Sleeping regains my vitality. I find that getting enough sleep helps me stop dwelling on negative thoughts and lifts my mood. It is my go-to method of self-care anytime I felt like I had fallen short of the standards I had set for myself. I also fall asleep when I'm bored, particularly on the bus ride to and from school.

Eating

Who wouldn't enjoy the experience of eating? When I'm stressed, bored, or upset, the first thing I do is always eat. Consuming food stimulates me and makes me want to get stuff accomplished. When I go too long without eating, I always find myself in a grumpy attitude. People often feed me before they have crucial conversations with me so that I'll be in a good mood to listen to whatever they have to say.
